ffjpeg

ffjpeg is a fast, minimal command‑line utility for compressing, resizing, and converting images. Built for developers who need precise control over image quality without heavyweight dependencies.

Download ffjpeg Features 
$ ffjpeg input.jpg cursed.png --quality 1

ultra low quality

$ ffjpeg bliss.jpg --colors 256

resize images perfectly

$ ffjpeg photo.png --resize 12000x8000
High-performance single-binary CLI
Supports JPEG, PNG, BMP, GIF, TGA, WebP, AVIF
Configurable compression and color depth
Flexible resizing up to ultra-high resolutions
No ImageMagick dependency
Designed for automation and pipelines
